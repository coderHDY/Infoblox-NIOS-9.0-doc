1. Click **Start** -&gt; **All Programs** -&gt;** Administrative Tools** -&gt; **DNS.**
2. Click **+** (for ns2) -&gt; **+** (for Forward Lookup Zones) -&gt; [**corpxyz.com**](http://corpxyz.com).
3. Right-click [**corpxyz.com**](http://corpxyz.com), and then select **Properties** -&gt;** General**.
4. On the *General* page in the [*corpxyz.com*](http://corpxyz.com) *Properties* dialog box, enter the following:
   
   - **Zone** **file** **name**: corpxyz.com.dns
   - **IP** **address**: Enter **1.1.1.2** and then click **Add**.
   - In the IP Address field, select **1.1.1.3** (the NAT IP address of the legacy DNS server), and then click **Remove**.
