1. Click **Start** -&gt; **All Programs** -&gt; **Administrative Tools** -&gt; **DNS**.
2. Right-click **ns3**, and then select **Properties** -&gt; **Advanced**.
3. On the *Advanced* page in the *ns3* *Properties* dialog box, clear the **Disable recursion** checkbox.
