1. Click **Start** -&gt; **All Programs** -&gt; **Administrative Tools** -&gt; **DNS**.
2. Click **+** (for ns1) -&gt; **+** (for Forward Lookup Zones) -&gt;** **[**corpxyz.com**](http://corpxyz.com).
3. Right-click [**corpxyz.com**](http://corpxyz.com), and then select **Properties** -&gt; **Zone Transfers**.
4. On the *Zone* *Transfers* page in the [*corpxyz.com*](http://corpxyz.com) *Properties* dialog box, enter the following:
   
   - **Allow** **zone** **transfers**: Select this.
   - **Only** **to** **the** **following** **servers**: Select this.
   - **IP** **address**: Enter **10.1.5.2**, and then click **Add**.
