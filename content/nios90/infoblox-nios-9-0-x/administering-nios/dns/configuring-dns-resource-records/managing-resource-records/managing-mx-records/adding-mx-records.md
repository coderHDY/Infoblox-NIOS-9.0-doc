To add an MX record from the Tasks Dashboard, see as described in [*Add MX Record*](/nios90/infoblox-nios-9-0-x/using-the-grid-manager-interface/dashboards/tasks-dashboard/ipam-task-pack). You can also add MX records from the **Data** **Management** tab -&gt; **DNS** tab by clicking **Add** -&gt; **Record** -&gt; **Add** **MX** **Record** from the Toolbar.
