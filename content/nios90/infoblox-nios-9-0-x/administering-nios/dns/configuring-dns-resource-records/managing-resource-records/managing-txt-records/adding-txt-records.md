To add an TXT record from the Tasks Dashboard, see [*Add TXT Record*](/nios90/infoblox-nios-9-0-x/using-the-grid-manager-interface/dashboards/tasks-dashboard/ipam-task-pack). You can also add TXT records from the **Data** **Management** tab -&gt; **DNS** tab by clicking **Add** -&gt; **Record** -&gt; **Add** **TXT** **Record** from the Toolbar.
