---
title: "Restoring Reclaimed Records"
source: "/space/nios90/1394575228"
pageId: "1394575228"
---
A reclaimed record remains in the Recycle Bin until the bin is emptied. You can restore the deleted records from the Recycle Bin, as described in [*Restoring Objects from the Recycle Bin*](/nios90/by-id/1466859853).

The Recycle Bin does not display information on whether a record was deleted during a scavenging process or manually.

Therefore, you cannot restore the reclaimed data only.

When a record is restored from the Recycle Bin, its Reclaimable flag is reset to "No".

> **warning**
>
> ### Note
> 
> Only a super user can restore records reclaimed during a recurring scavenging task.
