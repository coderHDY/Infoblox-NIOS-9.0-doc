You can view a list of IPv4 and IPv6 RIR networks in the **Data** **Management** tab -&gt; **IPAM** **tab** or the **Data** **Management** tab -&gt; **DHCP** tab -&gt; **Networks** tab -&gt; **Networks** section. For more information, see [*IPAM Home*](/nios90/infoblox-nios-9-0-x/administering-nios/ip-address-management/managing-ip-addresses/managing-ipv4-networks/ipam-home) and [*Viewing Networks*](/nios90/infoblox-nios-9-0-x/administering-nios/dhcp/managing-ipv4-dhcp-data/configuring-ipv4-networks/viewing-networks).
