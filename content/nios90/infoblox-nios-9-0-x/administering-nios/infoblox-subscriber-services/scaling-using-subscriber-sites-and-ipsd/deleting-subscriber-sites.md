source: "/space/nios90/1471480016"

---
To delete a subscriber site, complete the following:

From the **Data Management** tab -&gt; **DNS** tab -&gt; **Subscriber Services Deployment** tab -&gt; **Subscriber Sites** tab, click the Action icon next to the subscriber site name and select **Delete** from the menu.

In the *Delete Confirmation (Subscriber Site)* dialog box, click **Yes**.
