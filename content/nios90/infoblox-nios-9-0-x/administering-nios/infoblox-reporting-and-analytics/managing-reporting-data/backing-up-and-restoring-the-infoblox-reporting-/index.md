---
title: "Backing Up and Restoring the Infoblox Reporting and Analytics App"
source: "/space/nios90/1356890320"
pageId: "1356890320"
---
To back up the Reporting and Analytics App, go to the **Infoblox** **Grid** tab, select **Backup** -&gt; **Grid** **Backup** -&gt; **Manual** **Backup** from the Toolbar, and then select **Infoblox** **Reporting** **&** **Analytics** **App** to back up the app. When you back up the Reporting and Analytics App, the backup file is a .bak file that contains the reporting settings configured in the Grid Reporting Properties.  
To restore the Reporting and Analytics App, go to the **Infoblox** **Grid** tab, select **Restore** -&gt; **Restore** **Grid** from the Toolbar, and then select **Infoblox** **Reporting** **&** **Analytics** **App** to restore the app.
