> **warning**

> **Note**
> 
> This dashboard will be deprecated from NIOS 9.1.0 onwards. For future reference, use [*DNS Infrastructure Protection Top Rules Logged Dashboard*](/nios90/infoblox-nios-9-0-x/administering-nios/infoblox-reporting-and-analytics/predefined-dashboards/security-dashboards/dns-infrastructure-protection-top-rules-logged-d).

The *Threat Protection Top Rules Logged Dashboard* is a predefined custom dashboard comprising sub-dashboards listed in the table below. Duplicates of these sub-dashboards exist under new names, replacing "Threat Protection" with "DNS Infrastructure Protection" since NIOS 9.0.8. Dashboards with the previous names will be deprecated from 9.1.0 onwards.

| **Sub-Dashboard with**   **Previous Name** | **Sub-Dashboard with**   **Changed Name** |
| --- | --- |
| [Threat Protection Top Rules Logged](/nios90/by-id/1856045627) | [*DNS Infrastructure Protection Top Rules Logged*](/nios90/by-id/1429242918) |
| [Threat Protection Top Rules Logged by Source](/nios90/by-id/1858241115) | [*DNS Infrastructure Protection Top Rules Logged by Source*](/nios90/by-id/1428849707) |
