To save a search as an alert:

1. From the **Reporting** tab, select the **Search** tab.
2. Enter the search criteria and then click the Search icon.
3. From the **Save** **As** drop-down list, click **Alert**.
4. In the **Save** **As** **Alert** dialog box, specify all alert settings. For information about creating scheduling alerts, see [*About Alerts*](/nios90/infoblox-nios-9-0-x/administering-nios/infoblox-reporting-and-analytics/about-alerts)*.*
5. Click **Save**.
