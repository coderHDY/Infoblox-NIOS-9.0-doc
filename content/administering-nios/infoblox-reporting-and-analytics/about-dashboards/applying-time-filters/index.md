You can generate a dashboard for a specific time interval by applying time filters. You can filter results by preset time ranges, create custom time ranges, specify time ranges based on dates or date and time, or work with advanced features in the time range picker. For information about Time range picker, refer to the Splunk documentation.  
The date and time displayed in the **Time** filters are based on the time zone set in your user profile by default. For more information about how to configure a time zone, see Setting the Browser Time Zone in [*Setting Login Options*](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/318210233). However, the timestamp displayed in the results for a dashboard is based on the time zone configured on the reporting server.

> **warning**
>
> ### Note
> 
> The NIOS reporting data is updated at a certain time interval, rather than updating continuously. Therefore, the **Real-time** option in the **Time** filter might not work for most of the dashboards. For information about update time intervals, see Reporting Indexes and Update Time Intervals in [*About Reports*](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/280400960).
