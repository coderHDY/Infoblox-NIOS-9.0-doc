To add a CNAME record to a forward-mapping or reverse-mapping zone from the Tasks Dashboard, see [*Add CNAME Record*](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/280408558). You can also add CNAME records from the **Data** **Management** tab -> **DNS** tab by clicking **Add** -> **Record** -> **Add** **CNAME** **Record** from the Toolbar.
