To add an MX record from the Tasks Dashboard, see as described in [*Add MX Record*](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/280408558). You can also add MX records from the **Data** **Management** tab -> **DNS** tab by clicking **Add** -> **Record** -> **Add** **MX** **Record** from the Toolbar.
