To add an TXT record from the Tasks Dashboard, see [*Add TXT Record*](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/280408558). You can also add TXT records from the **Data** **Management** tab -> **DNS** tab by clicking **Add** -> **Record** -> **Add** **TXT** **Record** from the Toolbar.
