After the ability to generate reports for hardware appliances as well, three alerts are generated instead of one. The 'Flex Grid Activation' and 'Flex Grid Activation for Managed Services' licenses have their own set of alerts. The alerts filter members based on ReportingSPLA extensible attribute value and therefore, the ib-dns-usage-report-per-month alert may not generate data.
