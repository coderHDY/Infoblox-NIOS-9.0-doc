---
title: "Limitations of IB-FLEX"
source: "/space/nios90/1432787299"
pageId: "1432787299"
---
- IB-FLEX and standard shapes licenses cannot be added on the same node. However, mixed IB-FLEX and standard shapes licenses can be added in the same Grid.
- An IB-FLEX instance will not start if you do not configure the required minimum level of resources.
- The resources assigned to IB-FLEX for vCPU and memory must be equal to or exceed the minimum designated values for the platform. For more information about IB-FLEX platforms, see About IB-FLEX Instances and Platform Settings below.
