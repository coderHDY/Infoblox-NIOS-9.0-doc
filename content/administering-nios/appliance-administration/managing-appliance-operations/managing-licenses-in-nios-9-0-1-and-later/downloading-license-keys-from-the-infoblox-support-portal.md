You can download the license information from the [*Infoblox Support*](https://support.infoblox.com/) portal either in CSV or TXT format.

To download:

1. In the Support portal, use any of the following options:
   
   - On the **My Products** tab -> **Manage my host and software bindings** tab → **Hosts** tab -> find and select the license entitlement and click the **Download License File **button,   
     or, on the *Manage Software for Host* &lt;*host name*&gt; page > click **Download license keys**.
   - To download the DDI license information for all your appliances at a time, click **Download All DDI License Keys** that is available on the **My Products** tab -> **Hosts** tab.
2. In the *Download License Keys* dialog box that opens, complete the following steps:
   
   1. Select between **TXT** or **CSV** format.
   2. Click **Download**.
      
      *[image: media]*
   3. In the *Save as* dialog box, choose the location where you want to save the file.
   4. Click **Save**.
