title: "Obtaining Member Licenses"
source: "/space/nios90/2340487189"

---
Member licenses are mapped to the hardware IDs (also called as serial numbers) of individual NIOS or vNIOS appliances. Member licenses are termed as Static licenses in NIOS Grid Manager. They cannot be transferred among appliances. They come pre-installed on physical NIOS appliances according to the software packages you order at the time of purchase.  
When you install a new license with an expiration date beyond the existing license, it replaces the existing license. You can view their status in Grid Manager.
