1. On the **Data** **Management** tab, select the **DHCP** tab -> **Networks** tab -> ***10.1.1.0/24***, and then click **Add** -> **DHCP** **Range** from the Toolbar.
2. In the *Add* *Range* wizard, complete the following: ** **  
   **Start:** **10.1.1.10 ** ** **  
   **End:** **10.1.1.50**
3. Click **Next**, and then select **Server**. Grid Manager displays [ns3.corpxyz.com](http://ns3.corpxyz.com) as the assigned member.
4. Click **Save & Close**.
5. In the **Networks** tab, click ***10.1.2.0/24***, and then click **Add** -> **DHCP** **Range** from the Toolbar.
6. In the *Add* *Range* wizard, complete the following: ** **  
   **Start:** **10.1.2.10**  
    **End:** **10.1.2.50**
7. Click **Next**, and then select **Server**. Grid Manager displays [ns3.corpxyz.com](http://ns3.corpxyz.com) as the assigned member.
8. Click **Save & Close**.
