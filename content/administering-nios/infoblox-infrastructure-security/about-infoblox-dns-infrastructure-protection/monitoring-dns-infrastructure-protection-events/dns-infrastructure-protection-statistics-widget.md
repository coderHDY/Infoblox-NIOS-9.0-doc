You can also get a high-level view of the DNS infrastructure protection events through the Status Dashboard. The Advance Appliances provide the *DNS Infrastructure* *Protection* *Statistics* widget so you can monitor the trend and counts of the various events. For more information about the Dashboard and the *DNS Infrastructure Protection* *Statistics* widget, see *[Status Dashboard](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/537002114)*.
