NIOS provides a series of reports to monitor and analyze DNS Infrastructure Protection events. When you integrate a reporting member in your Grid, you can get the DNS Infrastructure Protection both hardware and Software DNS Infrastructure Protection related reports so you can monitor event counts by severity, member, rule, and rule category. Note that all reporting traffic goes over the MGMT port on the reporting member by default. If for any reasons you need to configure a different interface for the reporting traffic, you can do so through the reporting *Member* *Properties* editor. For detailed information about these reports and how to configure the interface for reporting traffic, see *[Security Dashboards](https://infoblox-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/nios90/pages/280760447)*.
